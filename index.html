What is mozilla

  • Mozilla is a free software community, created in 1998 by members of Netscape
  • The Mozilla community develops, spreads and supports Mozilla products, thereby promoting exclusively free software and open standards, with only minor exceptions
  • Some of the products developed by Mozilla include Firefox Web browser, Thunderbird E-Mail Client, Firefox Mobile Web Browser, etc.

Mission of Mozilla

Our mission is to promote

  • Openness
  • Innovation
  • Opportunity on the Web

Who are Mozpacers?

We are a nonprofit, volunteer run community comprising of enthusiastic Mozillians, FSAs (Firefox Student Ambassadors), FSA club leads and ReMos(Mozilla Representatives), working actively to promote free and open web, supporting community promoting our mission not limited to any area or locality.

Mozilla Delhi is one of the active communities of Mozilla in India.

WoMoz community in Mozpacers

  • The WOMOZ project is mainly dedicated to improving women's visibility and empowerment
  • Mozpacers being an active part of Mozilla community are committed to promote women's involvement in Mozilla
  • We strongly believe in equality of sex, religion or community

Mozpacers' mission

We build upon Mozilla's mission to make the web accessible to everyone and believe that, Internet is for the people, by the people and of the people. The Mozpacers community is actively contributing to create awareness about open source softwares and open source communities.

Mozpacers community structure

Community Manager
Community Managers mentor the community members about the course of action
Owner
An Owner owns volunteer teams and any conflicts inside or outside the team are to be resolved by owners
Lead
A lead is a splendid contributor in a team and has excellent knowledge about the subject
Core Contributors
Group of volunteers who are consistent in their contribution
Intern
An Intern is a contributor who has recently joined the team or who is a contributor but not consistent
