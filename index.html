What is mozilla

MOZILLA is a free-software community, created in 1998 by members of Netscape. The Mozilla community uses, develops, spreads and supports Mozilla products, thereby promoting exclusively free software and open standards, with only minor exceptions.
Mozilla produces many products such as the Firefox web browser, Thunderbird e-mail client, Firefox Mobile web browser, Firefox OS mobile operating system, Bugzilla bug tracking system and other projects.

Mission of Mozilla

Our mission is to promote

  • Openness
  • Innovation
  • Opportunity on the Web

At Mozilla, we’re a global community of technologists, thinkers and builders working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web.

What is Mozpacers

It is a nonprofit, volunteer run community comprising of enthusiastic Mozillians , comprising of FSAs ( Firefox Student Ambassadors) , FSA club leads and ReMos (Mozilla Representatives ), working actively in and around Delhi/NCR to promote Mozilla’s Mission .

Mozilla Delhi is one of the active communities of Mozilla in India.

SOP


This SOP has to be followed by all the core contributors of Mozpacers.The intention of SOP is to develop clear guidelines for the community so that everyone is aware of Do’s and Don’ts of the community.


  • Rules for Communication
    To make sure any communication doesn’t lead to an improper path, we follow a specific guidelines for communication.
  • Rules for Leave
    We make sure leaves do not affect the community hence we declare our leave in advance. Community leads also need to declare their leaves in advance.

  • Rules for Contributor of the Month
    The purpose of this award is to motivate the contributors and not to de motivate the others.

  • Rules for Peer Appreciation Award
    Peer appreciation award is given to the best contributor of the quarter. This award is on the basis of the consistent contributions.

  • Rule to become a SIG Lead

    SIG Leads are nominated by community leads on mutual decision. A contributor who thinks he/she is ready to take a SIG Lead position can approach any of the Community leads and express his/her interest. Decision would be based on the mutual discussion among community Leads. The minimum requirement to become a SIG Lead is 3 months of consistent contributions as a Mozpacer or vouched Mozillian with documented proof of his/her contributions.
  • Rule to become a Community Lead
    The minimum requirement for becoming a community lead of Mozpacers is at least 18 months as a vouched Mozillian and 1 year as Mozpacer. The candidate should be a mentor in one of the projects of Mozilla.

  • Rules for Office Hours
    All the core contributors should have an office hour defined for each of them and they should be available for interaction with all the community members at that time. Slack, being our official channel has been set up as a parameter for Office Hours.

  • Rules for Trello Task Responsiveness
    If one has been assigned a due date more than 5 days for a task, it is important for .him/her to provide some status of the task in the period of 5 days.
