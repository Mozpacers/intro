What is mozilla

MOZILLA is a free-software community, created in 1998 by members of Netscape. The Mozilla community uses, develops, spreads and supports Mozilla products, thereby promoting exclusively free software and open standards, with only minor exceptions.
Mozilla produces many products such as the Firefox web browser, Thunderbird e-mail client, Firefox Mobile web browser, Firefox OS mobile operating system, Bugzilla bug tracking system and other projects.

Mission of Mozilla

Our mission is to promote

  • Openness
  • Innovation
  • Opportunity on the Web

At Mozilla, we’re a global community of technologists, thinkers and builders working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web.

What is Mozpacers

It is a nonprofit, volunteer run community comprising of enthusiastic Mozillians , comprising of FSAs ( Firefox Student Ambassadors) , FSA club leads and ReMos (Mozilla Representatives ), working actively in and around Delhi/NCR to promote Mozilla’s Mission .

Mozilla Delhi is one of the active communities of Mozilla in India.

WoMoz community in Mozpacers

The WOMOZ project is mainly dedicated to improving women’s visibility and empowerment.
Mozpacers are committed to promote women's involvement in open source communities. We strongly believe in equality of sex, religion or community.Also, there is a rapid growth in the number of Womoz contributors in our Community within a very short span of time.
